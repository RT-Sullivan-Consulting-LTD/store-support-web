Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

Herdcount is developed by RT Sullivan Consulting Ltd, United Kingdom.

This policy describes what data the Herdcount app collects, uses, and shares. The short version: none.

Data we collect

Herdcount does not collect any personal data, usage data, or device information. The app has no network connection and no server. Nothing is transmitted anywhere.

Data stored on your device

Herdcount stores count records (label, category, count, notes, thumbnail, and timestamp) in a private local database on your iPhone. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any external server (it may be included in your local iTunes/Finder backup or iCloud device backup, both of which are controlled entirely by you)
  • Can be deleted at any time by deleting individual records or uninstalling the app

Photos and camera

Herdcount requests access to your camera and photo library solely to let you select a photo for counting. Photos are processed on-device and are not stored beyond the thumbnail you see in your count history. No photos are uploaded or shared.

Third-party services

Herdcount uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporters. The only third-party component is the YOLOv8n machine learning model, which is bundled inside the app and runs entirely on-device.

Children

Herdcount does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. No data is collected from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future app update, the updated policy will be published at this URL and the "last updated" date above will change. We will not introduce data collection practices without updating this policy.
